About WebchatAid

Welcome to WebchatAid, a dedicated platform focused on supporting the mental health and well-being of students.

Our Mission

At WebchatAid, our mission is to provide a safe, inclusive, and supportive space for students to explore mental health awareness, access valuable resources, and build resilience throughout their academic journey.

Our Intended Function

We made this website with the intention to helps elleviate the students mental health back to the average mental fortitude of the average student. The function of this website is to be a learning space, a space where students can get help for there mental health.
